How to Play

  • One night lasts about 10 minutes (12 AM → 6 AM on the clock).
  • Door — Only one entrance. Close it when something is in the hall. Closed door drains extra power.
  • Hall light — Check the corridor before the door. Drains power while held.
  • Cameras — Nine feeds (CAM 01–09). Track four animatronics. Monitor open drains power.
  • If something enters while the door is open, you will not get a second chance.
  • Pause — Pause button, P, or Esc (Esc first closes the cameras). Resume, Sounds on/off, or Main Menu from the pause panel.
